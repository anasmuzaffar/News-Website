UAE Unveils 2024 Festive


On Tuesday, the UAE Cabinet greenlit the official public holiday calendar for the upcoming year, applicable to both government and private sectors.

  • UAE Cabinet approves official public holiday calendar for the upcoming year
  • Applicable to both government and private sectors
  • Unified list ensures equal time off for employees

The unified list guarantees equal time off for employees. The comprehensive holiday schedule, posted by the government, includes dates determined by the Hijri Islamic calendar, with corresponding Gregorian dates subject to moon-sighting.

Embark on a year-long celebration in the UAE as the nation unveils its vibrant tapestry of public holidays and festivals! From the grandeur of national events to the cultural richness of religious observances, the festivities promise a kaleidoscope of experiences.

Whether you’re captivated by the dazzling displays of the fireworks during national holidays or immersed in the traditional charm of festivals rooted in the Hijri Islamic calendar, the UAE’s calendar is a testament to its diverse and inclusive spirit.
